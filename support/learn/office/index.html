Installing Office on your Personal Computer

Learn how to install and setup Microsoft Office for your personal/home computer.

Toggle Image Visibility

Step One

Open your internet browser and navigate to office.com and sign in.

Step Two

Click on the Install Office dropdown and select Office 365 apps. This will download the Office installer.

Step Three

Find the launcher in your downloads and open it.

Step Four

Once the app opens, it will ask you to stay online while it installs and activates Office. You may be asked to sign in with your school provided account and password.

Step Five

You're all set. Your Office apps will appear in the Start Menu! You can open and use your apps.

Step One

Open the macOS App Store and search for Office. Locate the Microsoft 365 app bundle. This contains all of the Office apps for macOS.

Step Two

Click Get and sign in with your Apple ID if prompted.

This download will take a long time, and requires over 4GB of storage. Please be patient while it installs. You may use your computer while you install.

Step Three

All of the Office apps are now installed! Open up an app and sign in with your school account email and password.

Step Four

You are ready to use your school provided Office 365 Subscription on macOS!

You should be signed in on all apps now.
